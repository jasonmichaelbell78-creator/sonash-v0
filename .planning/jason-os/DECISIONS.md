## Research Structure & Governance

| #   | Decision                     | Choice                                                                                                                           | Rationale                                                                                          |
| --- | ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 23  | Multi-phased per domain      | Each domain starts with scoping what to research within it, then executes                                                        | Research-about-research step prevents wasted effort on wrong questions                             |
| 24  | Gating protocol              | Checkpoints within each domain AND at domain completion, comparing against all prior findings                                    | Not just "did we answer questions" but "does this change what we thought we knew"                  |
| 25  | Orchestration method         | Structured git-tracked artifacts (RESEARCH_ROADMAP.md + SYNTHESIS.md + DEPENDENCIES.md)                                          | No new skill to build (rabbit hole risk). No GSD overhead. Disciplined artifact updates            |
| 26  | Cross-locale persistence     | All orchestration artifacts git-tracked and committed after every meaningful update                                              | State files and memory are supplementary, not source of truth. Git is the cross-locale bridge      |
| 27  | Sequencing approach          | Foundation first (archaeology + landscape + existing work), then domain-specific (sync weighted highest, UX deferred toward CLI) | Can't research extraction without knowing what exists. Can't design without knowing what others do |
| 28  | Priority weighting           | After foundations: sync #1, then domain-specific. UX deferred until closer to CLI phase                                          | Sync is the stated #1 pain point. UX matters most when CLI is being designed                       |
| 29  | File structure               | .planning/jason-os/ for planning artifacts, .research/jason-os/NN-domain-name/ for research outputs                              | Centralized under jason-os namespace. Numbered directories suggest sequence                        |
| 30  | Domain numbering             | Numbered (suggesting order, not rigid), insertable as NN-a for new domains                                                       | Flexibility to reorder while maintaining suggested sequence                                        |
| 31  | Reassessment triggers        | (a) Contradicting findings, (b) new domain needed, (c) every 3rd domain regardless                                               | Three triggers ensure the roadmap stays current without constant rework                            |
| 32  | Research completion criteria | No artificial limits — operator decides when research is sufficient                                                              | Contrarian and verification gates surface quality. Done when operator says done                    |
